Quick Care Tips

  • Light: Match plants with their ideal lighting conditions.
  • Water: Avoid overwatering by checking soil moisture regularly.
  • Humidity: Increase humidity with misting or a humidifier.
  • Repotting: Repot plants when they outgrow their containers.
